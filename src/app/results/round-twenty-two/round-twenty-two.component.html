Round {{ ROUND }}: Coin Spin

Spin a coin for as long as you can. This can be any normal coin (preferably a penny, nickel, dime, or quarter) and can be done on any open surface. As soon as you let go of your coin, the time starts. As soon as it comes to rest, your time stops. You may not touch the coin after it starts spinning. You may practice as much as you want.
