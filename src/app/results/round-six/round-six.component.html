Round 6: Do you know how long a minute is?

In this challenge, you must send a video of yourself counting to 60 seconds (one minute) with your eyes closed or blocked off. (No audio aids or any other BS either, unless it's coming from your own mouth or elsewhere on your body.) The idea is to get as close to an actual minute as possible. Your score will be the difference between your perceived minute and an actual minute. For example, if I count to 60 in 64 seconds, my score is 4.0 seconds. If I count to 60 in 56 seconds, my score is 4.0 seconds. You don't need to announce every counting number aloud, but you do need to say "start" to begin, and "60" or "stop" or "done" to finish and you do need to video the whole thing so that I can see how close to an actual minute you are. Team with the lowest average score wins!
